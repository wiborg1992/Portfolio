About me.

I'm a tech-focused Master's student with a background in law enforcement, working at the intersection of IT, AI and digital strategy.

Scroll

Growing up far from the tech world might not be the typical route into IT, but it teaches you to think fast and adapt. Before moving into technology, I spent several years in a specialised law enforcement unit, making high-pressure decisions long before dashboards and data models became my everyday tools. A lot has changed since then, but the mindset carries over navigating complexity, analysing situations quickly, and staying calm when things get messy.


Today, I’m an IT-focused Master’s student with a deep interest in AI, digital strategy and complex systems. Over the past years, I’ve worked on projects that translate organisational needs into clear, actionable digital solutions. Whether it's structuring chaotic data, analysing system dependencies, or making technology more intuitive, I enjoy turning complexity into something teams can actually use.


These days, I bring together analytical discipline, structured problem-solving and hands-on execution. I thrive where technology, strategy and human needs meet — breaking problems down, making them understandable, and helping organisations move forward with solutions that are both smart and practical. And yes, I still keep a healthy respect for AI that behaves itself.
